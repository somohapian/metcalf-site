ACTIVATE

Activate your fractional recruiting partnership.

Three monthly subscription tiers. No per-hire commissions. Pick the tier that fits the hiring volume and onsite support you need.

Activation is the start of a Metcalf Search partnership: choosing a tier, defining current open roles, setting comp bands, and beginning active sourcing in the first week. Most partnerships launch within seven days of activation, with first-round candidate conversations starting in week two.
CHOOSE YOUR TIER

Pick the partnership that fits.

Recruiting Partner for one active role at a time. Partner Plus for two to three concurrent roles and dedicated weekly onsite time. Partner Pro for full embedded recruiting across the company.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

After you activate.

Three things, in this order, within the first business day.

01
Confirmation and welcome

You will land on the onboarding page immediately after payment, with a kickoff call scheduler and the partnership materials waiting for you. A confirmation email arrives within minutes.

02
Kickoff call within 48 hours

A 90-minute intake where Jay learns your business, your open roles, your hiring history, and the candidates you wish you had hired. This call sets the search direction.

03
Sourcing begins immediately

Within five business days of the kickoff, the first wave of vetted candidates is in front of you. Search continues weekly until every role on the partnership is closed.

Have a question first?

If you want to talk through which tier fits before activating, send a note. A reply usually comes within one business day.

QUICK QUESTIONS

Before you activate.

What does the monthly subscription include?

Every tier covers sourcing, screening, interview coordination, offer construction, and retention support for every open role across the partnership. Tiers differ by pod size and search bandwidth, not by method.

Are there per-hire fees or commissions?

No. The monthly subscription is the full cost. There is no per-hire fee on any tier.

Can I change tiers later?

Yes. Tiers can move up or down at any monthly cycle. Rates are grandfathered, so a tier change does not reset the rate you are paying for work already running.
