Three tiers. Flat monthly fees. A single point of contact backed by their recruiting pod.

A fractional recruiting partnership is a flat monthly subscription that covers sourcing, screening, interview coordination, offer construction, and retention support across every open role. The work happens inside the client's hiring process, not outside of it. We deliver this through three tiers: Partner, Partner Plus, and Partner Pro.

What is in a Recruiting Partner

A Metcalf Search Recruiting Partner is a flat monthly partnership that handles five things across every open role: sourcing, screening, interview coordination, offer construction, and retention support. The five map to the STACK Method, the framework we use to describe how small businesses hire well. The services below are what that framework looks like when someone is doing the work.

Sourcing

Sourcing is finding the people you want to hire, which is almost never the same group of people who apply to your job posting. Most SMB hiring failures begin here. The wrong pipeline produces the wrong candidates no matter how well you interview. Sourcing inside a Recruiting Partner means active outbound to passive candidates, employee referral activation, targeted LinkedIn and trade community work, careers page traffic capture, and re-engagement of warm candidates from prior searches. Posting on Indeed is part of this. It is not most of it. See Source for the full picture.

Screening

Screening is the first conversation, and it is the most undervalued step in SMB hiring. A good screen filters for fit on the three or four things that actually matter and surfaces the two or three concerns worth raising before the hiring manager spends an hour on a video call. We screen every candidate before they reach you. You see fewer people. The people you see are closer to the role. The framework that drives the screen lives on Assess.

Interview coordination

Interview coordination is the part SMB owners do not think they need help with and then quietly hate doing. Scheduling across four interviewers, two candidates, and three weeks of competing calendars is its own job. It is also where Tempo dies. Candidates ghost when a process drags. A Recruiting Partner includes the scheduling, the candidate communication between interviews, and the debrief structure that keeps the process moving.

Offer construction

Offer construction is detail work on a clock: total compensation framed clearly, no-surprises offer conversations, and a sequence that does not leave the candidate alone with their current employer for a weekend. None of it is complicated. All of it has to happen in order, on time, in the same week you are running a company. The full pattern lives on Close.

Retention support

Retention support is the work that happens after the hire signs. Onboarding scaffolding, 30-60-90 planning, manager check-in cadence, and early warning signals when someone is drifting. The first ninety days decide whether a hire settles in for years, and the check-ins that make that happen are simple to plan and easy to lose track of. We keep them on the calendar. The mechanics are on Keep.

Tiers, briefly

Three flat monthly tiers: Recruiting Partner, Partner Plus, and Partner Pro. They vary by role volume and onsite time, not by quality of hire. Pricing is set in the first conversation. There is no per-hire fee on any tier, and rates are grandfathered when you move between tiers.

What is not included

A Recruiting Partner does not include payroll administration, benefits brokerage, employment law advice, performance management, or HR generalist work. Those belong to your HR person or fractional HR partner. Fractional Recruiting sits next to HR, not on top of it.
